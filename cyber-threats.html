Understanding Cyber Threats

Malicious actors use various techniques to compromise systems, data, and personal privacy.Cyber threats refer to any malicious act that seeks to damage, steal, or disrupt digital data, services, or systems. These threats can range from phishing scams and ransomware to more advanced persistent threats carried out by organized cybercriminals or state-sponsored actors. Understanding the nature of these threats is crucial in developing a proactive defense strategy. Recognizing common attack vectors, such as deceptive emails or unpatched software, can significantly reduce your risk. Educating users and implementing layered security can prevent most threats from succeeding.

  • Phishing: Fake emails tricking users.
  • Ransomware: Encrypted files for ransom.
  • Malware: Viruses, spyware, etc.
  • DDoS: Overwhelming services.
  • Zero-Day: Newly discovered exploits.
